Welcome to Shinchan’s World!

Where being naughty is a full-time job and underwear jokes are considered an art form. Get ready for laughs, weird dance moves, and total madness—Shinchan style!

About Shinchan Characters

Shinchan Nohara

Shinchan is a mischievous and funny five-year-old who loves chocolates, action figures, and annoying his parents. He's known for his unpredictable behavior and humorous comments. Despite his antics, Shinchan has a good heart and often surprises others with his actions. He is the main character of the series and beloved by fans worldwide.

Misae Nohara

Misae is Shinchan's mother. She is a housewife who tries her best to manage the household while keeping up with Shinchan's troublemaking. She's loving but often short-tempered, especially when Shinchan causes chaos. Despite that, she always supports her family and is the glue that holds them together.

Hiroshi Nohara

Hiroshi is Shinchan's father, a hardworking salaryman who often gets caught in his son's hilarious antics. He’s humorous, a bit lazy at times, and loves his family deeply. He often finds himself in awkward situations thanks to Shinchan, but takes everything in stride.

Himawari Nohara

Himawari is Shinchan’s adorable younger sister. She’s obsessed with shiny things and handsome men, even as a toddler. Although she can’t speak fluently, she communicates well and is just as mischievous as her brother. Her antics add another layer of humor to the family dynamic.

Shiro

Shiro is Shinchan’s loyal and intelligent pet dog. He’s quiet, kind, and often acts as a silent observer of the Nohara family's chaos. He’s often the only sensible one in the house and is deeply loved by Shinchan. His white fluffy fur and expressive eyes make him a fan favorite.

Nene-chan

Nene-chan is Shinchan’s bossy but caring friend. She loves playing house and gets upset easily, especially if things don’t go her way.

Bo-chan

Bo-chan is quiet, slow-talking, and always calm. Known for his runny nose and philosophical thoughts, he’s a unique member of the group.

Kazama-kun

Kazama-kun is smart, ambitious, and sometimes snobbish. He dreams of success and often gets annoyed by Shinchan’s silly behavior.

Masao-kun

Masao-kun is shy and sensitive, easily frightened and emotional. Despite being timid, he’s a true friend to everyone in the group.

Ai-chan

Ai-chan is a rich and polite girl who attends the same kindergarten. She has a crush on Shinchan and often behaves maturely for her age.

Midori Ishizaka

Midori Ishizaka is one of Shinchan’s nursery teachers. She's calm, responsible, and tries hard to manage the class with patience and grace. She eventually marries her long-time boyfriend.

Ume Matsuzaka

Ume Matsuzaka is another teacher at the kindergarten. She's stylish, bold, and always dreaming of romance. She's often at odds with Miss Yoshinaga over silly things.

Masumi Ageo

Masumi Ageo is a kind and quiet nursery school nurse. She rarely speaks but is respected by everyone. She's gentle with kids and acts as a calming presence at the school.

Bunta Takakura

Bunta Takakura is the grumpy yet caring headmaster of Shinchan’s kindergarten. He often appears strict, but deep down he loves the children and supports the teachers.

Nanako

Nanako is Shinchan’s teenage crush. She’s pretty, sweet, and treats Shinchan like a little brother. He is completely smitten with her and always tries to impress her.
